Phase Two ¶

The establishment of libertas DAO ¶

Libertas DAO will be established and governed by the community members. The community members will be granted:

  • Decide and vote on the theme of the next project
  • Libertas Fund will purchase land in Sandbox/Cryptovoxel and build a home for US and for our art
  • Host offline exhibitions in XX+ galleries globally
  • Collaborate with other crypto artists
  • Exclusive online and offline gatherings
